How it works

Three steps. None of them spreadsheets.

Sign in with Google

One tap. No password to forget at the squat rack. Your data lives on a single small server — no third-party analytics, no ads.

Tap reps as you lift

Each set is a big circle. Tap once when you finish it. Long-press to mark a partial. The rest timer pops up at the bottom and stays out of the way.

Let Train bump the bar

Five clean sessions in a row and your next workout is heavier. No need to remember what you lifted last Tuesday — Train already did.

Open it on your phone. Start lifting.

Train is built for an iPhone in your pocket between sets. Pair Bluetooth headphones, queue up a playlist, sign in once, and the next thing you need to do is press the bar.
